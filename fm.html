About Priyank

Priyank Sukhija is a prolific restaurateur, industrious to the core and a true innovator! A simple man, he doesn't sweat of success. An amiable personality, he is zealous towards what he does.
He never studied restaurant management and the art of creation and running a business comes naturally to him. "I am my own competition," he says. He is a true dreamer. He believes in following his heart and making a life on his own. His fascination towards design and interiors has lead to him investing heavily in the interiors of his restaurants.
For Priyank, his inspiration comes from traveling and he is a meticulous person as we can gauge from his endeavours so far. He loves doing what he does. "To do it for as long as I can, that is my vision for myself".



We are a team based in New Delhi, India. We are dedicated to creating a one-of-a kind experience for our customers who visit our restaurants at various metropolitan cities across India. We are here to revolutionize the F&B industry! Making a global debut soon.
